Moonshine Vision Lab

MVL is a research group at Sungshin Women's University, led by Dr. Oakyoon Cha. We study visual perception and cognition, with an emphasis on ensemble processing. Ensemble processing operates at various stages of visual processing (those associated with attention, perception, and memory) and with a broad range of visual stimuli (from simple bars and shapes to complex objects such as faces and cars), and so does our research.
